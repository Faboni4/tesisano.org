About the Series

Discover the world of Tesi & Sano and the mission behind the adventure.

A Tale of Hope

"Tesi & Sano" is more than just a cartoon; it's a call to action wrapped in a heartwarming adventure. The series follows Tesi, a spirited young fox, and Sano, a wise owl, as they travel across a changing world. From melting ice caps to drying rivers, they witness the effects of climate change firsthand.

But it's not all gloom! Along the way, they meet communities—both animal and human—who are working together to restore balance. They learn that even the smallest creature can make a big difference.

Core Themes

Climate Change

Explaining complex environmental issues in a way that is accessible and non-frightening for children.

Community

Showing that we are stronger when we work together. Unity is key to solving global problems.

Responsibility

Teaching that we all have a role to play in caring for our planet, no matter our age.
